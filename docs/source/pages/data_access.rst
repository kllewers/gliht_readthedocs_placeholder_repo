Interactive Webmap
=====================

G-LiHT data can be browsed and downloaded through the interactive webmap at
`glihtdata.gsfc.nasa.gov <https://glihtdata.gsfc.nasa.gov/>`_. The webmap lets
you visualize available flight lines and campaigns and download the products
you need.
