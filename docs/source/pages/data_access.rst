=================
Accessing Data
=================

Interactive Webmap
=====================

G-LiHT data can be browsed and downloaded through the interactive webmap at
`glihtdata.gsfc.nasa.gov <https://glihtdata.gsfc.nasa.gov/>`_. The webmap lets
you visualize available flight lines and campaigns and download the products
you need.

.. note::

   The webmap is under active development. If you run into issues or have
   feedback, contact the G-LiHT team (see :doc:`introduction`).

LP DAAC Archive
==================

G-LiHT data products are also archived and distributed through the NASA Land
Processes Distributed Active Archive Center (LP DAAC), searchable via `NASA
Earthdata Search <https://search.earthdata.nasa.gov/>`_.

Citing G-LiHT Data
=====================

If you use G-LiHT data in published work, cite the relevant LP DAAC data
product DOI(s) along with the foundational instrument paper:

    Cook, B.D., et al. (2013). "NASA Goddard's Lidar, Hyperspectral and
    Thermal (G-LiHT) Airborne Imager." *Remote Sensing*, 5(8), 4045-4066.

Questions
============

For questions about data access or the webmap, contact the G-LiHT team
referenced on the `G-LiHT GSFC site <https://gliht.gsfc.nasa.gov/>`_.
